Virtual CFE Exam Review Courses
(8 August - 8 September, 2021)

ACFE Oman is organizing a CFE Exam Review Course during the period from 8th August to 8th September 2021. This course is designed to review material required to pass the CFE Exam. Independent study with the CFE Exam Prep Course prior to attendance is strongly recommended.


The CFE certification by the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE) is considered as the golden standard of excellence in the anti-fraud profession worldwide. By becoming a CFE, you will gain knowledge to prevent, detect, and deter fraud inside your organization, as well as conduct effective fraud examinations. Other benefits include the following:
